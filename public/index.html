Not caring about tomorrow, always just seeking to have fun, we're all profoundly dreaming of staying kids forever. For a whole year, Florence and myself, CP, tried to show what living and going out in Montreal was like. We carried disposable cameras everywhere so we could snap those unique moments and so we could be able to share the actual feeling of it. No high-end tech or post-editing. Only films and the crappiest flash.

Enjoy our different sets or simply hit random for a randomly built set. The site was built to represent the feeling of looking at printed 4x6in photos. Receiving them in a random order and stacking them in a pile. Drop a beat from the player and scroll through the pics. I hope you'll remember the good times, but mostly, never forget that we're all born as kids.


-Flo & CP
